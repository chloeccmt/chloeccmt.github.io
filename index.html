The main goals of the assignment were to effectively use rhetoric, stasis theory, and technical writing techniques to reach other professionals or the public. ENGCMP0600 Safety Document

Students were tasked to create a safety document using Adobe InDesign. The main goals of the assignment were to create a document that was appealing and informative while using technical writing qualities. I created a veterinary surgery safety document. Please view my safety document above.

engcmp0600 Infographic
